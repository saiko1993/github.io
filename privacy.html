Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

LiquidGlassVault ("the App") is designed to securely store personal information such as passwords, API tokens, and sensitive data directly on your device.

Your privacy and data security are our highest priority.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers.

All information entered into the App — including passwords, API keys, tokens, card details, and notes — is stored locally on your device only.

3. Data Storage & Security

All stored information:

  • Remains on the user's device
  • Is not transmitted to any external server
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is not used for analytics or tracking

The App may use Apple's built-in encryption mechanisms and biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) to protect your data.

4. No Data Sharing

We do not sell data, share data, track users, use third-party advertising, or integrate analytics SDKs.

5. Internet Access

The App does not require internet access for its core functionality.

6. User Control

Users may add, edit, or delete stored data at any time. Deleting the App will permanently remove all locally stored information.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by modifying the "Last Updated" date above.
